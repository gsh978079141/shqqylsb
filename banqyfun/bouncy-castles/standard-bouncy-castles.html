Bouncy Castles

Bouncy castles for sale

The standard bouncy castle, with dimensions 5.2m x 4.0m has the perfect size for commercial use. This standard sized inflatable has been preferred by hiring companies and venues since the early 90’s. Standard bouncy castles offer enough space for larger crowds, but they are still easy to install and to handle. These inflatables can even be used for smaller parties and venues, both indoors and outdoors.

Bouncy Castles in the UK

Our standard bouncy castles can be installed, packed up and transported by just 1 person. The price of our bouncy castles include a (Gibbons) fan, a set of pegs, a safety certificate (EN 14960), a transporting bag, a manual and a unique 5 year warranty. JB-inflatables has service stations in Leicester and Essex where repairs or warranty issues can be handled. If you want to know more about our truly unique warranty policy click HERE.

We are not your typical bouncy castle sales company. We do not just sell you a bouncy castle, but we will be there for you after your purchase as well. When you buy a bouncy castle from us, that is just the beginning of our relationship. We will help you grow your business and improve your skill set.

Just sign on to our newsletter, read our blogs and follow us on Facebook. We will keep you updated about developments in the industry, new innovations, useful tips and promotions.
